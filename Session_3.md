(Pickle+delivery 11 cr; finder's fee 2 cr.)

A document contractually required to exist — and the paper trail Bill's angles wanted.
8. **The third path identified.** Pickering pays the WALL outright (3 kilocreds raisable; wall 1,950). Bill enters as a paid provisional, signs only a fee receipt. License + money + survival reconciled. Bill commissioned the poison pill (−100 cr) and authorized the runner-word **"Tuesday."**

Doss corrected: **the chair IS the contract**; no seat floats free of the writ. **Sponsorship = adoption** (sponsor answers for the man); she won't sponsor a 2-day acquaintance with a lawyer, an unaccounted purse, and an unexplained refusal — and clocked the money question (rail-money, or something she can't see). Reframed: the story is the SILENCE, not the hole; a licensed man above ground working the cover-up. Spent ONE thread — **the fictitious inquiry** (6-men-wide, safe from Rusk) — and told Pickering to re-ask the widows *"what were you told about why you couldn't say."* Invoked the wall both directions. **Pickering signed** (T. Pickering), funds inside the week, left believing the refusal a favor, off to re-interview six widows. His read: the first source he'd believe against his own eyes.

Tatch returned: **Esther will meet at the mission side-yard bench, 10 AM**, and sent *"I already know it's bad… tell him to come anyway."* Min reports **Rusk safe and lit** in his usual corner this evening.
