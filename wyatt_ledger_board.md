## ⚙ QUEST INSTRUMENTS — the inventions bench (NEW, Day 17)

- **THE HAND-WARMER — PROVEN.** Reasoned it out of the morning fire watching Eli's red hands: hurried rust is a slow burn without a flame. Iron filings + brine (salt urges it along) + bone-black (porous — holds the damp, carries the warmth) + a little sawdust for body, in a double cloth pouch (coarse in, fine out), kept dry till use then opened to air. **Bench-proved Day 17:** one proof charge first (it WARMED — the idea's real), then a heat-vs-duration matrix. The dial's lawful: lean = mild and long; heavy = hot and short; the blister ceiling found at the very heavy end (safe band well below it). **Working ratio circled in the work notebook.** THREE problems still open: (1) the warm-up's too SLOW — finer iron, looser pack; (2) PACKAGING — it needs air to run, so dry-till-use is a real seal-and-open puzzle, untouched; (3) IRON SUPPLY — hand-filing's trials-only, a batch wants sweepings by the pound. A Quest Instruments product. Pearce would find it worth his time — bring it PROVEN, not as an idea.
- **THE SPEEDLOADER — DESIGNED, building Friday.** For my gun (a TOP-BREAK — the gunsmith read it Day 17): throw all six spent clear on a sharp break (part technique, part a tuned extractor), then a rim-gripping holder — spring-brass first — that drops six fresh into the open cylinder in one blind motion, cut to THIS cylinder's rim-circle. The release matters more than the grip; it lives or dies on letting go clean and blind. Gunsmith says it'd genuinely work, hasn't seen one. **Build it with him Day 19.** Patentable, like the catch — another bright thing; mind the count.

## ⚠⚠ The hypothesis — why clean works (mine, undeveloped)

Miasma doesn't fit. Beatrice eased the fever by BOILING cloth and changing the WATER, not the air. My read: something unseen, alive, living in filth and water, carried body-to-body by touch, killed by heat, kept off by clean water. The books have the right habit (cleanliness) and the wrong reason (smell). Acting on it: boil the makings AND the vessels; mind water sources. The test (deferred): a microscope, three waters. **The microscope's in Pearce's dead kit** — gated on him, a season off. Keep it quiet — a tinker theorizing past the doctors is interesting to a watcher.
