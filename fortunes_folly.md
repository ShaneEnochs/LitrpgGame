# FORTUNE'S FOLLY — the crew

*Recurring NPCs (Bill's delve crew). **Mechanical data — stats, gear, abilities, credits — lives in `fortunes_folly_crew.json`.** Per-member warmth + ceiling live in `bill_weaver_sheet.json` → `relationships` (Myles / Charles / Jason / Julie). This file holds voice, relationship texture, and key exchanges. They know Bill as **Wyatt**, named in for real S28. They are friendly, competence-respecting, and don't care about his cover — but a named crew ties his alias toward four delvers (exposure to track).*

## Myles — tank / leader
**Texture:** the steady center; reads Bill as a man who already knows what he did and isn't asking to be impressed (his highest regard). Calls the line; Bill executes. **Voice:** talks little, decides fast, never panics. *"I bring my crew home."* **Defers the opening read to Bill on every floor** (a standing habit since the Emberwaste).

## Charles — healer
**Texture:** the conscience of the crew — the one who says turn back and patches them anyway. The **newest of the four** (joined on their 2nd Platinum). Prayed for exactly a range gun, and Bill is the answer; the warmest toward Bill of the four. **Voice:** dry, fatalistic, 50s. *"Wyatt is my favorite person on this crew."* His "nobody bleeds" is the standing aim (matters doubly with the Ash Rats' blood-trigger). Carries the heal — Bill carries light.

## Jason — damage / fighter
**Texture:** the crew's edge and its risk; Julie's older brother; warmed to Bill immediately. **Voice:** hot, fast, grinning, late 20s; loves Platinum and the work. Loose under fire (*"remind me to stand behind you"*). The spear-tip who dives flanks and gets pulled out.

## Julie — damage / mage
**Texture:** the planner, closest to Bill's temperament; warms once he proves he listens. **Voice:** cool, precise, mid-20s; the brains and temper-brake to Jason. **The open master class on active conversion** — Bill learned the continuous open draw off her at the Floor-2 rest (S27), skin-contact read, one sitting; she runs her own filter at ~75% (her blind cap). Standing offer to keep teaching; her *"ask me again when we're out, clean"* door is open. Permitted Bill to read and log her Arcane density-knot (S27).
