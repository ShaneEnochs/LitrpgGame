## Story so far (compressed)

Bill Weaver — true name held by very few — came to New Abilene as "Wyatt," a forgettable fix-it man, carrying a real purpose under the cover: he means to understand the dungeon at Hollow Reach, and the wrong machinery moving under this frontier, without being seen doing it. Over nineteen days he has built a life dense enough to stand on: a discreet children's firm (Tatch coordinating, Min on the books) that now earns its own keep on standing income; a furnished house held through a trust with no Weaver name on it; a lawyer (Calloway) holding everything that could hang him; a newspaperman (Pickering) bound by a confidentiality promise and a poison-pill account; and a quiet web of trades and teamsters who know him as competent and dull.

The Hollow Reach baseline — the only "before" that will ever exist — was measured on Day 7, surface only, never past the mouth. Crew 4 went down after; the surfacing of what comes back up is the heavy thing still owed. Bill's father Malcolm answered the wire in their idiom: a 5,000-cr gift (cleared whole, in trust) and a separate structured 5,000-cr loan now arriving in staggered parcels. The firm has begun branding and distributing Quest Remedies; the first distribution run and a camp run have shipped through the subcontractor structure. Bill has taken up the gun (Crane), the knife (Marguerite for the cut, Dell for the fight), and a string of inventions that carry his hand — a patented lift catch, a now-workable hand-warmer, a designed speedloader, a solved-on-paper self-feeding stove — against the standing discipline to *build dull*. The school wish, planted with Sister Beatrice, reached Father Lessard's table on Day 18 and became a real mission the priest will carry to his Bishop.

**Day 20:** a full town-side day that turned heavy at the hall. Calloway's: the receipt slip lodged in the iron box; the **NW-land conflict beat** fired (Calloway declined "land matters northwest," conflicted, no specifics — banked as a watch); and Bill, seeing his single-point-of-failure, decided to **open a trust-owned bank box** for the poison-pile originals (Calloway arranging; the schedule-split held open pending his sorted list). The gunsmith's: the **reserved extractor timing cut taken and the fault closed for good**, then the **speedloader real build founded** — body and six matched, positively-indexed seats cut. The hall: the **crew-4 surfacing finally fired** through the writ channel as texture (Doss reading it cold against her clock-discrepancy unease; Quint pressing for access) — and Bill answered by **choosing to file his Reach survey NAMED**, spending nineteen days of dull-fixer cover deliberately; Doss revised him upward and **set a guild survey for first light Day 21**. Bill also **gained Doss as a monster-fighting teacher** (reading a monster by *what kills it / what it's about to do*, constructs-as-mechanisms, the line to the door).

**Day 21 (this session — the day everything turned):** the named Reach survey day became Bill's **awakening day.** Riding out on the guild conveyance, a fresh, off-map **special Foundation delve** surfaced on the road; Bill entered it SOLO and cleared it. First kills (construct sentinels, then a forge-gatekeeper) **opened his core** and booted his System — **Class: System Engineer, Lv. 1.** Inside he took the intrinsic **Forceread**, cultivated on the forge's concentrated essence (packing his core dense and **holding his first gate deliberately unopened** — the patient play), and recovered a haul crowned by a **class-resonant great forge-core** the System flags as a bindable growth item. The completion granted a second intrinsic, **Schematic**, and identified that core. Then he rode on and **completed the named Reach survey** surface-only over the Day-7 baseline: every reading worse and worsening toward the mouth (colder, flame-starved-and-blue, wrongness spreading), and — now awakened — he *felt* the mouth **drink essence** toward a center past a **warm, strained, tended seal** holding something that pushes, the draw leaning toward his own full core. He filed it NAMED but gave it to **Doss behind a shut door**, where the two became **trusted allies**: he laid out the whole human-layer picture (Esther's missing brother Amos; the watcher; cage-laundered hush money; the Esther payoff) and his "it takes people and gives back something not-quite-them" thesis, and Doss **corroborated** (Garlan reads "wrong" to her; the clock discrepancy = a cheerful report from men already gone). They agreed a survival posture (survey buried as leverage; triage the people over the slow walled hole; protect Esther + Rusk; Bill the hands, Doss the guild-eyes; never seen together). Bill learned **core suppression** off Doss in one sitting (found his own weave; banked himself from a ringing beacon to a banked fire), registered the haul (the great core nameless until an assayer — routed to **Pearce**), and went home to sleep, owing a morning re-registration. **Major worldbuilding was locked this session** (the maker-core's purpose; essence aspects/filtering/banking/cores; the local-delve/recruitment economy — see Cultivation/abilities and the truth files).
