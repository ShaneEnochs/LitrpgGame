## Story so far (compressed)

Bill Weaver — true name held by very few — came to New Abilene as "Wyatt," a forgettable fix-it man, carrying a real purpose under the cover: he means to understand the dungeon at Hollow Reach, and the wrong machinery moving under this frontier, without being seen doing it. Over eighteen days he has built a life dense enough to stand on: a discreet children's firm (Tatch coordinating, Min on the books) that now earns its own keep on standing income; a furnished house held through a trust with no Weaver name on it; a lawyer (Calloway) holding everything that could hang him; a newspaperman (Pickering) bound by a confidentiality promise and a poison-pill account; and a quiet web of trades and teamsters who know him as competent and dull.

The Hollow Reach baseline — the only "before" that will ever exist — was measured on Day 7, surface only, never past the mouth. Crew 4 went down after; the surfacing of what comes back up is the heavy thing now owed. Bill's father Malcolm answered the wire in their idiom: a 5,000-cr gift (cleared whole, in trust) and a separate structured 5,000-cr loan now arriving in staggered parcels. The firm has begun branding and distributing Quest Remedies; the first distribution run shipped through the new subcontractor structure this week. Bill has taken up the gun (Crane), the knife (Marguerite for the cut, Dell for the fight), and a string of inventions that carry his hand — a patented lift catch, a proven hand-warmer core, a designed speedloader — against the standing discipline to *build dull*. The school wish, planted with Sister Beatrice, reached Father Lessard's supper table on Day 18 and became a real mission the priest will carry to his Bishop.

**Day 18 (this session):** a clean full day on Bill's own sequencing — Marguerite's third blade lesson (the trotter joint, one pass); two reloading sessions at the home bench; a snap-shot morning with Crane (Shooting 4→5); the gun-knife seam with Dell; a deep, paperless laying-in of cartridge components through the gunsmith ("my shelf, your cash"); and Lessard's supper, where "all the children, no line" passed the priest's table. The camp run shipped trusted to the firm. The crew-4 spine did not fire — no natural channel — and carries forward owed.
