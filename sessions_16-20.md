# ARC 4 — DAYS 16–20: THE QUIET EMPIRE, AND THE TURN
*(Consolidated, lore-corrected narrative of Sessions 16–20 and their close-outs. History layer — current values in the JSONs, current threads in `campaign_state.md`, NPC detail in `npcs/`. Bill is **unawakened** through the close of Day 20; the awakening is Day 21, the next arc. This arc ends on its eve.)*

---

## The throughline

Five days of consolidation that ended on a hinge. Bill proved his fall-arrest catch by standing under the cage himself, turned the firm self-sustaining, put the Quest Remedies line into real production, and spun off a string of clever inventions — then trained hard enough that the delve stopped being abstract. He won the school past a hard priest's gate, and on the last evening he spent the thing he'd guarded for nineteen days: he put his **name** on a Reach survey, and set the first-light trip that would change everything.

## The Dunmore demo, and the firm in the green

The lift-catch heat-treat was taught hands-on by Pearce (color reading, case-hardening, the quench instant, drawing the temper — *"Wait for the color, not your nerves"*), and Bill's own careful once-over caught a fault that had passed the forgiving trip-test: the wedge seated a hair off-square, which under a full hard-cut load would have crowded the shock to one side. He trued it, taught Cobb and Holt the lesson (*check hardest when you're sure*), and then **stood under the loaded cage and called for the rope.** The catch bit — a dead-level stop, a hand's-width of fall, no slip. The Dunmore lift-works job closed (265 cr), and the **three standing arrangements** locked: Remedies wholesale (six-month price-lock, the brother's margin climbing 3→4→5), gear-maintenance (80/mo to the firm treasury), and storage (5/mo).

On that, the **firm restructured into the green** — an independent **subcontractor of Quest** with its own name, work-for-hire contracts, the kids' own names on the paper, and a conflict-of-interest guard Tatch raised himself (when Bill is the customer, Tatch or Min signs for the firm — *"especially when you're being fair, 'cause that's when nobody's checking"*). Quest pays the firm 1 cr/tin to distribute. Min's read: between gear-maintenance and the tin cut, the firm now clears its wage bill on standing income alone — **at or past self-sustaining.** The first full **Remedies production run** (58 tins) poured clean and went out on the first distribution run, and the kids' **winter coats** finished (eleven, youngest-coldest first).

## Training for the delve, and the seam

Bill began Doss's training conditions in earnest. Crane (the range) and Dell (the yard) each gave real hours, and a lunch with Crane turned the whole effort **strategic**: the gun's job underground is *distance and the first moment* (snap-shooting from a bad start), reloading fast and blind is the survival skill (the man who dies is the one caught empty), and the real craft is the **seam** — knowing the moment to stop shooting and let the knife or the feet take over. Bill carried that framing to Dell, who taught the gun-to-knife transition both directions; the cross-draw turned out to *be* the seam made physical. Shooting reached the professional band (the pause-at-level flaw found and killed); the blade work stayed gated on the one thing no yard can give — cutting something that **resists**, the *"means-it"* gate both Marguerite and Dell named. *"Practice settles the hands, not the gut. The men who go looking are the men I bury."*

## The inventions stack up

Three more Quest Instruments candidates came off Bill's bench, each a known principle reasoned fresh, characterized, and built to **fail safe**:
- **The speedloader** — a rim-gripping holder for the (now-confirmed) **top-break** revolver, with a tuned extractor. A cheap-brass "mule" taught the real lesson — spring-brass fatigues and *betrays slow*, fine on day one and spilling six by day thirty — so the real build was re-scoped to better temper, a gentler bend, and replaceable fingers on indexed seats. By Day 20 the holder body and six matched seats were cut; the extractor fault closed for good.
- **The hand-warmer** — a flameless iron-rust burn (fine/coarse iron blend, salt, bone-black) in a two-part pouch: an air-tight inner barrier that keeps it inert in storage and a controlled-breathing outer pouch that meters air once keyed. A **workable prototype** — fails toward dead-in-storage, never toward a blister. One gate left: iron supply at scale (the dignified reason to send for Pearce).
- **The self-feeding stove** — a gravity-fed coal magazine, solved on paper against its one real danger (a starved overnight burn making invisible gas): a direct-draft bypass, damper and air stops that *can't* fully starve the fire, a simplified exhaust. It fails toward wasted fuel, not a roomful of gas. Unbuilt — and explicitly *not* to be installed in his own house (his most clever object pointed at his own address).

Each is a second, third, fourth attributable clever thing on the heels of the patent — exposure Bill is tracking against Calloway's and Maddox's warnings. The cartridge supply was laid in the quiet way too: the gunsmith carries a deeper shelf, Bill buys off it for cash ("my shelf, your cash"), no recurring order with his name on it.

## The school past Lessard's gate

At Father Lessard's supper (inside the mission Bill is **John**), the school wish became real. Lessard tested him — *eat first, don't pitch me* — then set three gates: sustainability (a teacher is a forever cost; promise a door then shut it and you've been crueler than never opening it), *which* children (the firm's own have a roof; the stockpens boys who belong to nobody are who the mission is *for*, and they come with trouble), and the Church layer (a mission school is the Bishop's). Bill refused the versus: **all the children or none** — sorting them rebuilds the same fence and calls it charity. His *why*, the most he's shown anyone in town: there are literal monsters in the world now, so the least a man can do is try not to be the other kind; twelve years on, men still sort and exploit each other when what comes out of the holes could lift everyone. Lessard stopped eating — *"you out-Scriptured a priest at his own table"* — heard the grief under it, and gave his verdict: *"all the children, no line — that I can carry to a Bishop. That is a mission."* And a warning held as a gift: **true is dangerous.** A sincere man who believes the world could be lifted and won't be is the most dangerous kind to those profiting from it staying down; men have been disappeared for less. *Bank the fire; keep the speech for tables where the company's already chosen.*

## The turn

Across these days the Coulter & Reese pressure sharpened — a rush party turned back off their parcels by armed company men (the *Clarion* pointing skepticism at the land for the first time), and a party quietly seeking Calloway as counsel for "land matters northwest" (he declined, conflicted). Bill split his single point of failure: the poison-pill originals move to a **trust-owned safe-deposit box** at the New Abilene bank (split custody, not counsel — Calloway stays whole counsel).

Then, at Doss's desk on Day 20, the long-owed **crew-4 surfacing** landed through the writ channel — a status reading *progress, resupply requested, crew sound*, dated sound the day before it was sent. Doss named her standing unease aloud and stopped herself: the clocks on this hole have never sat right — *someone keeps reporting good news on a schedule from that hole.* She named the **Quint pressure** (an eastern charter's advance man pressing for access under the Special-Delve classification, who'd love a clean recent survey calling the site quiet), and laid the filing-form cost straight without steering: verbal-only, written-unnamed, or written-**named**.

Bill chose, eyes open, to **put his name on the paper** — *"anonymity isn't worth the lives of others"* — spending nineteen days of dull-fixer cover and a town's goodwill on it. Doss revised him upward (*"most men spend a town's goodwill on themselves"*) and tied it to her own vise: a named engineer's filed evidence that a sealed mouth is *moving* forces the people above her to act or be on record ignoring it. **A guild survey was set for first light, Day 21** — on the guild's coin, a furnished rig with a driver and a hand, instruments from the cage, the finding to be filed **named** on return. She also agreed to become his **monster-fighting teacher** — gave the first hour on the spot (read the monster by what kills it and what it's about to do; Constructs read best by a man who reads machines; *know your line to the door and spend more attention on it than on the thing in front of you*) — "owed, for what you're spending tomorrow." The Rusk first-tick now sequences (the crew-4 result has reached the cell).

Bill went home through the deep cold, the revolver clean and full, and slept ahead of the first-light wagon — still, at the close of Day 20, an unawakened man.
