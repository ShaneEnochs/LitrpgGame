# RESOLVED THREADS — closed-outcome archive

*Threads genuinely closed in play, pruned from the living documents per §24, preserved here so the outcome is not lost. Outcome-only; the full detail lives in the dated `Session_N.md` pointer.*

**The sorting rule (read before adding anything here).** This file holds **closed events** — a thing that happened, finished, and will not re-open. **Anything that stands indefinitely stays in the living documents even if it "happened" in a past session** — protections, relationships, conditions, legal status, open agreements, and unspent forks do not become "resolved" just because they were set up once. When an instrument closes but leaves a live term behind (a signed trust with a standing condition; a recorded assay with an open fork; an executed contract with a continuing duty), the **event** is archived here and the **live term** is pointed back to `campaign_state.md`. Pointers run both ways. *(The poison-pill account is the clearest example of what does NOT belong here: it is permanently live protection and stays in `campaign_state.md`.)*
