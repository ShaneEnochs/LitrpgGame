## The throughline

Bill's father answered, and the answer rebuilt the board: a gift, a house, a name held in reserve. Around that, Bill turned a scattered set of trades into the **Quest** enterprise with real infrastructure — a roof, a partnership, a teaching plan, a treaty with Pem — and brought Pearce most of the way home. Then two mysteries arrived that the money couldn't buy off: an anonymous "friend of the work" who knows too much, and the open question of his brother Shaun.

## The father answers

Malcolm's keyed reply came through Calloway's channel — on its face a freight man's cold wire: *"QUEST. AGREED. NOT ONE CHARGE — SET IN PARCELS. SOUND GROUND FIRST. — Q."* Yes to the loan, paid in staged parcels (blasting idiom), caution handed over as method, signed Q — the cipher key and his mother's word for him, meaning *I know exactly who this is.* With it came two things the ask hadn't named: a **5,000-credit gift**, cleared whole into trust, separate from anything owed (a father answering the son he knew how to reach); and a **furnished house** bought in trust, no Weaver name on the deed, closing the same day because Calloway already held the right property.

Two trusts were locked, kept separate by design (*"separate the necks"*): the **children's trust** (Maddox sole trustee, for the firm's kids — it does *not* hold the house) and a **Quest trust** (Calloway trustee, Bill beneficiary) that **holds the house deed**. The house is Bill's — his asset, cost, and risk — and the firm has only the *use* of it; that first night it sheltered the firm's children warm, Tatch holding the key. Bill cut the Maddox tie clean (owing nothing, owed nothing) and moved his ciphered notebooks into the new house's upstairs window casing.

On the cover: Bill decided he will eventually **collapse to his true name — Bill Weaver, one man, openly — but last**, after the run of new things goes cold, so no watcher sees three remarkable arrivals on one man at once. For now the trust holds the deed and Wyatt is the dull tenant; the lease he signed carries Calloway's termination key and a bar on visibly improving the place past a workingman's means. The structured 5,000 loan stays deferred (~Day 17, arriving scattered — Calloway's seeded payoff still loaded).

## The Quest enterprise, built out

- **Quest Remedies.** The salve's warm-weep defect was fixed — the wax-to-oil base proved to be a *dial*, one controlled system yielding a firmer summer tin and a softer deep-cold tin. Branding settled: **Quest Muscle Balm** (comfrey) and **Quest Hand and Foot Balm** (yarrow), under a permanent struck *QUEST* die commissioned from the tinsmith (filed under "Wyatt"). Dr. Arrowood blessed the **first-aid kit** by mouth on his terms — clean-and-cover, *never treat*; the cleaning agent does the work the eye can't check; balms marked foolproof and never into an open wound — and will vouch it verbally to crews but won't print his name.
- **Quest Academy, designed.** Min's books were honest: the firm is not yet self-sustaining on the children's skills. So the plan is a **teaching relay** — Bill teaches the able kids who can then teach down the line; letters bound to a trade (want a trade, sit for letters); money carried as investment on Min's books, not charity; shifts that keep the watch lit through the rush. The clean outside teacher is the same person the school wants — a road that runs through Lessard's supper (Day 18).
- **The economic rule, set.** Bill's expert hands — certification, instrument bodies, gunsmith-grade work — earn *his pocket*; the firm earns off what the young ones make (edges, lamps, maintenance rounds, wheels). Min's books read truer for the split. The guild's **certification backlog** (the rush, one drowning certifier, the Aldine clearance idle three weeks) became a paying job Hessler set up.

## The Compact, settled with Pem

The two-outfit treaty Pem struck on Day 6 finally moved. Pem sent the rules; Bill answered. **Four base rules:** patches (each outfit's watched ground is its own; neither works the other's without the boss's yes); jobs and books (solo work whole to that outfit, shared jobs on one shared book Min keeps, the split named *before* the job); crews don't deal crew-to-crew (goods and word move boss-to-boss); disputes the two bosses can't settle go to Calloway — not to pick a winner, but to record what was actually agreed. **Two hard lines, both defensive:** no buyout ever (*"I been somebody's property and I'm done bein' it"*) and a clean exit (each walks out with what was theirs). Bill said yes to all of it, and asked Calloway only to write the *method* for splitting what gets built together during a season. Two independent outfits sharing one table — Pem an equal boss, never absorbed. (Tightened by Calloway, routed to Pem; pending-but-close. **Do not flatten this back to a "loot-share."**)

## The Dunmore lift-works partnership

Bill won the Dunmore Brothers' in-town freight-and-storage hoist job (re-scoped from the stale "mine two days out" posting; distinct from the boarding house where Esther rooms). He diagnosed the faults — a greased brake band, a racked guide shoe, a single hemp rope holding everything — and designed a **fall-arrest catch**: rail-gripping dogs that throw when rope-tension goes slack. Priced good-for-him (~265 all-in) and traded the premium for three **standing arrangements**, each handed back as the brother's own gain: **Quest Remedies distribution on the Dunmore freight runs, firm gear-maintenance** (the origin of the firm's standing Dunmore income), and **storage** of Quest's bulk goods off the house — all contingent on a **cut-rope proof** (Bill offered to stand under the loaded cage himself). The catch's novelty made it patent-worthy; Bill routed the patent through **Malcolm**, not Calloway — a keyed alert wire, drawings to follow by sealed letter, Bill the named inventor.

## Pearce turns the corner

Across passes three and four, the courtship moved from *talks to me* to *sees what was lost* to working partnership. Pearce read the lift design as a real assayer — caught that a spring cocked under load for a year loses its temper unseen ("a metal problem dressed as a mechanism problem") — and Bill's redesign answered it: weight-driven, self-feeding wedges that the cage's own fall drives tighter, the spring reduced to a nudge. Pearce engaged as a **paid metallurgy consultant**: at the smith he taught the judging (the ring on the anvil, the grain at a filed break, the spark for carbon) and sorted Bill's steel into *true* and *don't* — catching a bar that would have passed Bill's eye and drawn a fatal set under load. Asked to *teach* rather than be *used*, twice, he agreed to stand the heat-treat too — *"You've been square twice. I'll take the third on faith."* On the boxed library going to damp: *"Sell — no... lendin's a different word. I'll think on it. Get your lift standing and your metal read true first."* Not a yes; not a no. The deepest steps yet — his trade handed back on his own terms. (Drink first, books never first, building last still holds.)

## Two mysteries the money can't buy off

- **A friend of the work.** An anonymous letter came through the channel — inner sheet, **QUEST** on the fold — from someone who knows small-room facts: that Doss filed Bill into a drawer instead of onto a list, the surface-assessment cover, the gradient finding, the three watchers by their *functions*, the receiving channel itself. It states plainly it is **not** Sands or the watchers and stands *above* them — *"they watch because they are told to; I do not need to be told"* — knows that QUEST is Bill's *father's* word, urges Bill to keep measuring quietly (not for the office that capped his scope), offers contact on Bill's terms "the way your father taught you," frames itself as practicing a version of Bill's work "longer than you have been alive," signs only **"a friend of the work,"** and tells Bill to burn it. He boxed it instead. Calloway's read: three classes of knowledge — family, present-local, and the voice of an older *trade* that thinks of Bill's work as a craft with elders — that no single known party holds; the letter's function is to show reach and decline to use it, a card laid face-up; the danger is its gentleness, telling an isolated man *I see you and they don't*. (Identity DM-side; defers to the mystery anchors.)
- **The Shaun trace.** Bill named his brother to Calloway: **Shaun**, who knows QUEST, gone about four years after a degree in advanced mathematics, who asked the family not to look for him and said he'd return when ready. Calloway's read: the *wait-for-me* shape fits an ordinary falling-out *and* a brilliant man quietly acquired by something nameless — the same coat on both — and a mathematician's mind is exactly what powerful parties recruit; the trace would look both ways. (Calloway carries this thread on his own initiative thereafter.)
