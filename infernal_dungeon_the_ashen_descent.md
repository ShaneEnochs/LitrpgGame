## Core Systems

### Safe Rooms — The Charred Chapels
Scattered across all five floors are heavily warded safe zones called Charred Chapels: ancient ruined holy sites the infernal corruption couldn't fully destroy.

- **Mechanics.** Crossing the threshold instantly drops all mob aggro. Health and mana regenerate ten times faster. A party can rest here without triggering random encounters or ambushes.
- **The Bonfires.** Lighting the central bonfire in a chapel creates a checkpoint. Active bonfires can be warped between, skipping previously cleared floors.
- **Locations.** Usually hidden behind illusionary walls or guarded by elite mobs. Floor 4's chapel is suspended above a lake of liquid agony and requires solving a jumping puzzle to reach.
- **Single-entry caveat.** The chapels and bonfires work normally **inside** a run — rest, regroup, checkpoint, warp between cleared floors. They are not a way out and back: leaving the inner dungeon through the entry arch ends your one permitted entry. A chapel is where you regroup mid-run, not where you retreat to town and return.

### Dynamic Dungeon Events
The dungeon behaves as a living ecosystem; these trigger at random to force the party to adapt. Each is tied to a floor (detailed in the floor sections):
- **Ash Squalls** (Floor 1) — zero-visibility ash tornado, constant choking damage; find cover within thirty seconds or wipe.
- **Magma Tides** (Floor 2) — the lava lake rises ten seconds after a warning rumble; bridges submerge; climb and fight off flyers until the tide recedes.
- **The Executioner's Patrol** (Floors 3 & 4) — an unkillable Abyssal Juggernaut and its hound trackers roam; break line of sight and hide until they pass.
- **Spatial Fractures** (Floor 5) — corridors lose gravity at random; push off ranged-weapon or spell recoil to cross.

### Soul Coins
The dungeon's non-hostile NPCs deal only in **Soul-Coins** (a drop / valuable found inside; see Loot). They ignore ordinary gold and copper entirely.

## Floor 1 — The Smoldering Wastes

The entrance drops into a massive crater filled with knee-deep gray ash. Crumbling guard towers barely stand over rusted iron gates. The air tastes metallic and heavy with sulfur, and visibility is poor.

**Mob spawns**
- **Ash Rat** — hides in the deep ash and debris and ignores the party until a member drops to low health, at which point it swarms aggressively.
- **Cinder Imp** — snipes the party with weak fireballs from high in the ruined towers; panics and flees the instant it is engaged in melee, often aggroing nearby mobs as it runs.
- **Smoldering Skeleton** — mindlessly patrols the main path, swinging rusty iron swords, and bursts into a blinding cloud of hot ash when killed.
- **Coal-Goblin** — a cowardly hoarder lurking in the ruins; throws jagged rocks from a distance and tries to steal dropped loot off the ground before sprinting away.
- **Barbed Hell-Hound Pup** — hunts the open ash in packs of three to five, flanking the party and biting ankles to inflict a crippling slow.
- **Smoke Wisp** — drifts unseen in the ash haze, floating passively until sudden movement disturbs it, then envelops a victim's head for choking damage and zero visibility.

**Dynamic event — Ash Squalls.** The wind picks up into a zero-visibility ash tornado. The party takes constant choking damage and must reach structural cover within thirty seconds or wipe.

**Floor boss — The Charred Warden.** A towering molten jailer armed with a massive iron key, blocking the heavy iron elevator down. He seals all room exits the moment he aggros, then summons cages of fire that trap individual players. Break the cages manually before he winds up his room-clearing hammer sweep.

**Side quest — A Prisoner's Vengeance**
- *Trigger:* loot a skeletal arm still clutching a rusted iron key.
- *Objective:* find the Warden's holding block hidden in the ruins and release the trapped spirits before engaging the boss.
- *Reward:* the freed spirits strip away the Warden's heavy fire shield at the start of the fight.

## Floor 2 — The Obsidian Caldera

The elevator opens into a sprawling cavern lit by harsh orange light. Lakes of lava bubble violently, and the only safe footing is a network of razor-sharp obsidian bridges. Thick steam vents regularly obscure vision.

**Mob spawns**
- **Brimstone Slime** — lurks at the bridge edges to dissolve the party's armor and leaves highly acidic puddles behind when it dies.
- **Lava Beetle** — rolls into a fiery ball and charges across the narrow walkways, ricocheting off the walls; its goal is to knock players off the edge into the magma.
- **Flame-Weaver Spider** — hides in the stalactites, strings nearly invisible fiery webs to catch victims mid-fall, and shoots venomous spikes from relative safety.
- **Screaming Bat** — hangs still from the cavern ceiling, then dive-bombs players and lets out a sonic shriek that interrupts spellcasting.
- **Blood-Tick** — drops from the stalactites onto unsuspecting players, latches onto armor gaps, and drains health rapidly until a teammate forcibly removes it.
- **Ashen Ghoul** — rapidly regenerates while standing in fire; rushes in to grapple players and drag them toward the lava's edge.

**Elite / named spawns**
- **Magma Elemental** (elite) — ambushes from the lava falls if players harvest nearby brimstone; grapples targets to drag them under and splits into two smaller elementals at zero health. *(Tied to the side quest below.)*
- **Spitting Drake** (elite bridge-guardian) — a massive, flightless reptilian beast holding a chokepoint bridge; spews cone-shaped waves of acidic fire and uses its heavy spiked tail to knock melee attackers off the edge.

**Dynamic event — Magma Tides.** A low rumble warns the party; ten seconds later the lava lake rises and the obsidian bridges fully submerge. Scramble up vertical stalagmites and fight off flying mobs until the tide recedes.

**Floor boss — Broodmother Ignite.** A gargantuan demonic spider on a web of magma over a sheer drop. She refuses to pursue and instead pulls players toward the edge with sticky threads while infinitely spawning smaller flaming spiders. Burn her structural anchor webs to drop her directly into the lava.

**Side quest — The Alchemist's Greed**
- *Trigger:* harvest Brimstone Chunks near the lava falls; an elite Magma Elemental spawns.
- *Objective:* kite the elemental into a freezing trap **without killing it** to extract an intact Magma-Slime Core.
- *Reward:* high-tier fire resistance potions that trivialize the Broodmother's ambient heat aura.

## Floor 3 — The Corrupted Bastion

A heavy drawbridge crosses into a twisted demonic castle. Stained-glass windows depict horrific tortures; the floors are polished black stone; iron maidens and torture racks line the long, silent hallways. The temperature drops rapidly, replaced by a cold dread.

**Mob spawns**
- **Succubus / Incubus Scout** — patrols the halls, casting charm magic to briefly turn party members against one another, and attacks with a spiked whip if cornered.
- **Obsidian Gargoyle** — blends perfectly into the dark vaulted ceilings, then drops without a sound to pin squishy magic users to the floor.
- **Pain-Cultist** — conducts blood rituals in the side rooms, buffing allied demons and casting tracking blood-magic projectiles from the extreme backline.
- **Shadow-Stalker** — completely invisible in the castle's unlit halls; strikes the rearguard with poisoned daggers and instantly retreats back into the dark.
- **Horned Brute** — a slow, heavily armored guardian; charges in straight lines and uses massive club swings that permanently shatter weak shields.

**Elite / named spawns**
- **Iron Maiden Construct** (elite) — one of the hall's torture devices walking on its own; swallows a single party member whole and crushes them slowly while the rest of the team scrambles to break it open.

**Dynamic event — The Executioner's Patrol** *(also Floor 4).* A massive, unkillable Abyssal Juggernaut roams the halls with Hell-Hound Trackers. If the hounds catch the party's scent, a hunting horn blows; break line of sight and hide in a side room until the patrol passes.

**Floor boss — Kaelen, the Fallen Paladin.** A corrupted knight wielding twisted holy fire, waiting in the desecrated grand chapel. He dynamically switches between aggressive dual-wielding and heavy shield defense based on the party's positioning, actively parries physical attacks, and heals himself by sacrificing his own summoned angelic-demons.

**Side quest — Consecration**
- *Trigger:* find a shattered holy symbol in the armory.
- *Objective:* collect three vials of uncorrupted water hidden in the barracks and pour them over the chapel altar before Kaelen notices.
- *Reward:* locks Kaelen out of his ability to heal by sacrificing his demonic summons.

## Floor 4 — The Fields of Torment

The castle's back doors open onto a fleshy, organic labyrinth. Petrified Torment Wood trees scream continuously, rivers of Liquid Agony flow through deep trenches, and the ground pulses steadily underfoot.

**Mob spawns**
- **Soul-Eater Wraith** — completely ignores armor and drifts through the bone walls of the maze to drain the party's mana pools instead of their health.
- **Blood-Mage Fiend** — casts heavy area-of-effect curses from behind the maze's organic cover, temporarily flipping healing magic into damage, and shields itself with the lifeforce of lesser mobs.
- **Abyssal Juggernaut** — ignores the maze paths entirely, smashing straight through petrified wood and bone walls and destroying the terrain to reach its targets. *(A second one anchors the Executioner's Patrol, below.)*

**Elite / named spawns**
- **Pit Fiend Enforcer** (elite) — dual-wields extremely long flaming whips; specifically targets weapons to disarm front-line fighters and physically pulls casters out of the backline into melee range.

**Dynamic event — The Executioner's Patrol** *(also Floor 3, above).* The unkillable Abyssal Juggernaut and its Hell-Hound Trackers roam the maze; break line of sight and hide until they pass.

**Floor boss — The Amalgamation of Sins.** A shifting, formless mass of limbs and screaming faces guarding the final gate. It randomly adapts its elemental resistances every thirty seconds and absorbs incoming magic of the matching element to grow larger and hit harder, forcing casters to constantly rotate spell types. It also attempts to gain a massive speed buff fed by the ambient noise in the room.

**Side quest — Silence the Screams**
- *Trigger:* take direct sonic damage from a Torment Wood tree.
- *Objective:* burn five corrupted roots scattered around the maze.
- *Reward:* silences the trees and removes the Amalgamation's noise-based speed buff.

## Floor 5 — The Ashen Keep

A pristine, terrifying palace built entirely of void-obsidian. Floating infernal-iron structures hover above bottomless black pits. There is zero background noise, and the absolute silence is heavily disorienting.

**Mob spawns**
- **Chaos Weaver** — manipulates the keep's layout with heavy spatial magic, swapping player positions right before traps trigger and physically reshaping the rooms.
- **Infernal Inquisitor** — casts targeted silence zones to shut down casters, then teleports straight past the front line to hunt down and execute healers with a serrated sword.
- **Dread-Knight** — guards the massive throne-room doors, strictly challenging the strongest party member to single combat and ignoring all other targets unless provoked.

**Elite / named spawns**
- **Gorgon-Demon Hybrid** (elite, throne-approach guardian) — emits a continuous aura of extreme sluggishness and turns victims into smoldering stone statues if they hold eye contact for more than three seconds. *(This is the petrification Lady Sybilla's "Tear of a Penitent" cures — bring one down from Floor 4.)*
- **Hellfire Phoenix** (aerial elite) — soars above the keep raining down massive meteor strikes; revives from its ashes at full health unless the party destroys its egg within ten seconds.

**Dynamic event — Spatial Fractures.** The void-obsidian keep is unstable; corridors randomly lose gravity. Players float aimlessly and must use the recoil from ranged weapons or spells to push themselves to the next doorway.

**Dungeon boss — Azazel, the Ashen Sovereign.** The absolute ruler of the dungeon, seated on a throne of black iron.
- *Phase 1* — a methodical, stationary magic duel: overlapping geometric fire puzzles and delayed explosions.
- *Phase 2* — he shatters his throne to forge a massive obsidian greatsword, gaining aggressive teleport strikes and targeting the healer relentlessly. He also summons Elite Dread-Knights as backup during his final ten percent.
- *Desperation (10% health)* — the arena shrinks to a tiny circle of safe ground surrounded by a rapidly encroaching void.

**Side quest — The Broken Contract**
- *Trigger:* loot a Diabolic Contract from a slain Inquisitor.
- *Objective:* find Azazel's private study and burn the contract in the Infernal Brazier.
- *Reward:* nullifies Azazel's ability to summon Elite Dread-Knights during the final ten percent of his health bar.
